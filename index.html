MindSetting

At MindSetting, we are committed to helping you improve your quality of life by easing mental and emotional struggles and finding alignment. Having worked as a scientist, I understand the challenge of maintaining a healthy work-life balance, especially under uncertain and demanding circumstances. My own experience as a new mother, facing a dysregulated nervous system, poor sleep, and a tough transition back to work, deepened my passion for exploring women’s mental health and well-being.

To better serve others facing similar challenges, I pursued life coaching and nutrition certifications, building on my expertise in yoga, meditation, and my background in neurobiology. This journey ignited a deep commitment to supporting women, especially mothers, in finding balance, clarity, and fulfillment in their lives.

MindSetting is designed to help you rewire your brain and nervous system to work in your favour. Through personalized deep relaxation techniques, transformative conversations, and coaching exercises, I offer a holistic approach that nurtures both mind and body. Together, we'll work towards a more relaxed, aligned, and fulfilling life, giving you the clarity and confidence to take the next steps toward the future you envision.
